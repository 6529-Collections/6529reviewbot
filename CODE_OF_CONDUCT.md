# Code Of Conduct

6529reviewbot follows the 6529 expectation of serious, good-faith,
high-signal collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good faith, but be precise about risks and tradeoffs.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.
- Credit ideas and prior work.
- Avoid harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Abuse, harassment, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately derailing security or operational discussions.
- Using the project to target people, repos, or organizations in bad faith.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or PRs; block
users; or take other action needed to keep the project safe and useful.

Report concerns privately to a 6529 maintainer.
